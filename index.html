Hello there! I am an international student from Malaysia and this is my second year living in United States. I love food and coming from a multi-racial country, the States offer a vast different of food to try that I cannot found in my home country. I am taking my chance being here by expanding my cuisine experience so I could give recommendations to my friends and family when they are travelling. I like being at different places so I travel a lot too. Hope you are ready for an adventure!

Visit my Instagram page for more food reviews!

Here are some places I would recommend, and I will be reviewing what food I had while I was at these places.

Madison, Wisconsin
Ah! The capitol city of the state of Wisconsin. Probably my second home since I am going to school here. I spend my free time exploring different kinds of cuisines offered here. From Italian fine dining to a quick Chinese takeout, I would never get sick of the food here.
Portland, Oregon
One of my favourite places to go in Pacific Northwest region. The nature is beyond beautiful, the weather is just nice especially in the summer. Spoiler alert: you may find weird people, but they are good weird people. The last time I went here was summer 2023 but Portland, you still have my heart.
Seattle, Washington
Another state in Pacific Northwest you should go! The state parks nearby are so breath-taking and one of the gems in the States, but if you prefer the hustle-bustle of city life, Seattle is the perfect place! There are many cuisines you can find here, and I would recommend taking a walk a lot so you could appreciate the skyline view here.
